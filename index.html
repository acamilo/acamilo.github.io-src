MRI Compatible Robot Controller

These boards control piezoelectric motors used in MRI Compatible Surgical Robots. Working with the WPI AIM LAB I Designed, Routed, and assembled the Digital Circuitry on these cards.

Dynamic Input Output Controller

At Neuron Robotics, I designed, Assembled, and took to manufacturing a Robotics Educational product called the DyIO. It interfaces apps running on the PC to motors and sensors

Neuron Robotics

Founded by me and fellow classmates Neuron Robotics Targets the educational and research robotics with open source software and hardware in the form of the DyIO and Bowler Studio.

TNL One Cook Prototype

I Worked with Tech-No-Logic to prototype the brain of the the One Cook, a internet connected cooking robot demoed at CES.

CHDK-PTP-Java

The Canon Hack Development Kit firmware is an after market upgrade for many cameras. Among other features it enables Remote Control over USB. I wrote a library that allows a Java app to control the camera.
